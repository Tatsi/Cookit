Welcome to Cookit!

Cookit is a social community for food lovers. It tells you what you can cook from the ingredients you have. You just need to update your own ingredients when you buy something new. When you prepaire a meal just scale the recipe portions and press Cookit, our service will know what you used and substract the ingredients from your stash automatically.

In Cookit you can also create your own recipes, share recipes to Facebook, follow other users, favourite recipes and keep track of what you've been eating.

If you're still not sure if you want to join, just take a look at some tasty recipes created by our users below!
